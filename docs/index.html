What is LittleYcoin (LYC)?

An open-source peer-to-peer digital currency, favoured by Painted Turtles worldwide.

At its heart, LittleYcoin is the crypto movement that makes people smile! It is also an opensource peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that utilises blockchain technology, a highly secure decentralised system of storing information as a public ledger that is maintained by a network of computers called nodes. Its amazing, vibrant community is made up of friendly folks just like you!
